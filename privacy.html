Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026 · Effective: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Genext Information Systems ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use thailetters.com and the ThaiLetters.com learning tool (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Summary

ThaiLetters.com runs entirely in your browser. It is a flashcard tool for learning the Thai consonants, vowels, and numerals. We do not operate accounts or maintain databases of personal profiles. We do, with your consent, use third-party advertising and analytics, which may process limited data such as your IP address. The key points:

  • Your learning progress and preferences are stored locally in your browser, not on our servers.
  • Advertising and analytics cookies load only after you consent via our cookie banner.
  • We do not request your precise location, and we do not sell your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Learning data (stored on your device)

If you use features that remember your progress (such as which characters you have practiced or marked correct/incorrect) and your settings, that information is stored in your browser's local storage on your own device. We do not transmit or store it on our servers, and it is removed when you clear your browser data.

2.2 Technical and usage information

Like most websites, our hosting provider and the third-party services we call automatically receive your IP address, browser type, device characteristics, and timestamps as part of normal internet communication. If you consent to analytics, we may collect aggregated, non-identifying usage statistics (such as pages viewed and practice sessions started).

2.3 Cookies and similar technologies

We store your cookie-consent choice locally in your browser. With your consent, Google AdSense and analytics providers may set cookies and use similar identifiers. See our Cookie Policy for details and controls.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate the Service and present the Thai-alphabet flashcards and your results.
  • To remember your settings and progress on your device.
  • To display advertising that helps keep the Service free (only with your consent).
  • To understand aggregate usage and improve the tool (only with your consent).
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), California residents have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@thailetters.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law. If you are in the EU/UK, see Section 7.
